Stellar Laboratories Scientist Archive

Personnel Profiles

Dr. Nathaniel Stark

Background:

Dr. Nathaniel Stark is an expert in quantum mechanics and the study of dimensional anomalies. As the lead researcher of the "Stark Experiment," he sought to create a direct link to alternate realities by manipulating high-frequency signals.

Raised in a family of brilliant scientists, Dr. Stark's work was initially met with skepticism, but his results on quantum resonance quickly gained attention in the academic world. He led the team that discovered the Aether Signal, a bizarre and unexplainable transmission that seems to respond to human interaction in ways that challenge the very foundations of physics.

Dr. Stark's obsession with dimensional physics led to his involvement in the development of the **Stark Wave**, a machine capable of creating an artificial dimensional bridge. However, following his final experiment in 1994, he disappeared, leaving behind a trail of encrypted data and cryptic messages.

Dr. Alistair Nova

Background:

Dr. Alistair Nova is a theoretical physicist with a focus on the study of electromagnetic anomalies and the connection between matter and energy across dimensions. He joined Stellar Laboratories in 1991, bringing with him his groundbreaking research into the Aether Signal.

Dr. Nova's work primarily revolved around the "Nova Equation," a formula that allowed the team to track and amplify signals from unknown dimensions. His work enabled the team to extend the range of the signal detection systems, opening the door to further exploration of the Aether Signal and its influence on reality.

His collaboration with Dr. Stark led to major advancements in the lab’s ability to manipulate dimensional waves, though his theories often clashed with other team members, particularly regarding the implications of what they were attempting to achieve.

Dr. Vincent Calder

Background:

Dr. Vincent Calder was a systems architect and expert in the detection and analysis of temporal anomalies. His early work in AI systems made him a perfect candidate to design the laboratory’s experimental equipment that could monitor the "Residual Layers" left by dimensional shifts.

Dr. Calder's work focused on capturing the aftereffects of events that occurred in parallel dimensions or through shifts in time. His methods involved creating highly sensitive resonators capable of reading the echoes left behind by these occurrences.

Despite his relatively calm demeanor, Dr. Calder was known for his unwavering belief that reality itself was malleable, an idea he shared with Dr. Stark. His last communications were full of warnings about the dangers of interacting with the Residual Layers, claiming that their existence threatened to destabilize all of reality.

Dr. Nova Stellar

Background:

Dr. Nova Stellar, the enigmatic founder of Stellar Laboratories, was a visionary scientist who proposed that interdimensional travel was not only possible, but that humanity could unlock the secrets of the multiverse through the proper application of quantum mechanics and signal theory.

In 1989, Dr. Stellar founded Stellar Laboratories, a cutting-edge research facility designed to study dimensional anomalies and the potential for interdimensional communication. His belief was that technology could not only detect alternate realities but could also open doors to these dimensions, granting humans access to knowledge and resources beyond comprehension.

Unfortunately, his vision was seen as too radical, and many of his ideas were dismissed by mainstream scientists. In 1994, Dr. Stellar vanished without a trace during a secret experiment in which he attempted to establish direct communication with an alternate dimension. Some speculate that he succeeded in his experiment but became trapped on the other side.
